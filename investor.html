How it works

From social media noise to peer-reviewed truth.

A four-step automated pipeline runs every morning. No human intervention needed between trigger and publish.

1
Scrape trending claims
Reddit public JSON feed (11 nutrition subreddits) + GDELT global news monitor (65 countries, free API). Top 20 claims ranked by engagement velocity.
Reddit GDELT No API keys required
2
Score with Claude AI
All 20 claims sent in one batch to Claude (claude-sonnet-4-6). Returns score, verdict, global opinion, Brit Take, and cited sources. One API call, ~$0.50.
claude-sonnet-4-6 ~$0.50 per run
3
Publish to static JSON
Results written to data/daily.json on GitHub. Committed and pushed automatically. GitHub Pages serves it globally.
4
App reads live data
The web app fetches the JSON on every load. No server. No database. No backend costs. The whole stack is GitHub Actions + GitHub Pages + Anthropic API.

Revenue model

Four revenue streams. One intelligence layer.

The data pipeline is the asset. The app is the distribution. Every screen is a monetisation surface — but the trust is what makes them convert.

Yummy Score recipes drive purchase intent. Branded QR codes at checkout convert readers to buyers at Whole Foods, Sprouts, Ralphs. Retailers pay per redemption or monthly partnership fee.

Why now

Three forces aligning in 2026.

01
AI made real-time fact-checking economically viable
Scoring 20 claims daily against peer-reviewed science cost ~$500/day with human researchers in 2020. Claude AI brings this to $0.50/day — a 1000x cost reduction that makes the product viable at launch, not at scale.
02
Post-pandemic trust crisis in health information
COVID accelerated both health anxiety and health misinformation. 68% of adults say they don't know who to trust for nutrition advice. The gap between what goes viral and what's actually true has never been wider or more visible.
03
Retail media is looking for content-to-commerce bridges
Whole Foods, Sprouts, and every major health grocer is actively investing in content partnerships that convert engaged audiences to in-store buyers. We have the content, the audience, and the QR mechanism already built.
