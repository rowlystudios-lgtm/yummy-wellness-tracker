About
"What Yummy Wellness actually does."
We track trending health claims from Reddit and global news daily, then score every single one against peer-reviewed science. No agenda. No nonsense. Warm, honest, evidence-based.
How the score works
85+
Well supported by strong peer-reviewed evidence
40+
Partially true, overstated or context-dependent
0+
Misleading, false or unsupported by evidence
Where the data comes from
Daily pipeline scrapes Reddit nutrition communities and GDELT — a global news monitor covering 65 countries. Claims scored by Claude AI against peer-reviewed research. Editorial review before anything goes live. Refreshes at 6am EST.
